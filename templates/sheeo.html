SHE.E.O

Let's smash patriarchy, smash prejudice, empowering women entrepreneurs

'SHE.E.O' is Pepcorns initiative to level the playing field for female entrepreneurs. Inclusiveness and diversity are the only way forward to any progressive society. Our goal with She.E.O is to not only encourage a particular gender to build and scale their business, but also to handhold their peers and support them as well. We may blame dominance, but certainly it is not the answer. With She.E.O now you have more opportunities to raise funds, pitch among the like-minded community, earn initial traction, and perks of Pepcorns credits.

If you have any suggestions or feedback on how we can make She.E.O drive more exuberant then do let us know. mail us: team@pepcorns.com
