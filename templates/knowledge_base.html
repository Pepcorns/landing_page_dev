Process involved in investing in companies on Pepcorns

While building Pepcorns, our focus was on democratizing the startup funding with fast transaction deal flows. Traditional funding deals take months to finalize and execute. And so, we have made the process 'simple and minimal' with all necessary compliances.

Giving everyone access to startup investing, and levelling the playing field for startup fundraising.

  • Create an account: Sign up, and get your free account. Complete the required details to get started, and you're ready to drive the change!
  • Explore Startups: Browse from a curated list of startups, which are of your interest.
  • Do your own research: We recommend investors examine startups, check their market presence, look out for moat, and vertical they are dealing in. Make sure you understand the risks associated with the investment.
  • Invest: As you finalize the startup, choose your amount to invest and complete your commitment.
  • Wait for Perks: As startups achieve milestones, they disburse rewards. In the case of equity, they sign the term sheet and formalities. In either case, to earn well you need to wait and cheer them. In case, the company does not reach their minimum funding goal, all funds will be returned to the investors within 5 business days.

Cancelling an investment

You may cancel an investment under 10 days, from the date of investment you made. Remember, reducing your investment is considered as cancellation. For any such cancellation, you may click the given cancel button or write us at Investment@pepcorns.com

Can the startup reject my investment?

Yes, every company reserves the right to reject, in whole or in part, any investment commitment at any time before the proceeds are drawn from the escrow account. Any rejected investments will be returned to the investor in full.
