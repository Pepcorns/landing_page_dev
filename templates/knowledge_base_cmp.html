How it works?

Process involved in raising funds on Pepcorns

At Pepcorns our focus is to make startup funding fast, easy and affordable for all. With that thought, we have streamlined the process cutting major hectic so that founders can start the campaign in less than 20 minutes. We are continuously working to simplify the process further.

Steps to raise funds

  • Apply: Click on 'raise fund', fill the form and submit your application.
  • Diligence & selection: While we verify, we will be in touch with you to understand your business better. Help us with required information timely, so we can fast process. Complete paperwork and filing process.
  • Craft your campaign: Setup your account and campaign page â€” Create a company profile that provides an apt, concise overview of your company, product or service, and funding goals. Tell your story in your own words. Build and design your campaign page to attract investors.
  • Raise funds: While your campaign is up and running we recommend you approach customers, friends, family, and colleagues to invest in your business. It's crucial to build initial traction, otherwise why would any stranger invest in you if the people who know you best won't?
  • Payout rewards: After your campaign reach target, your funds remain safe with us in an escrow account, we wait for you to deliver rewards to your investors. This helps us be fair to both!
  • Receive funds: You receive the funded amount in your registered bank. Your company gets shifted to alumni.

Keep posting updates on your campaign page, this earns trust and encourages people to invest. Also, this helps the next round VC to approach you.

Who can fundraise?

Who can raise capital on Pepcorns?

Depending on the nature of business any startup seeking early fund (pre-seed/ seed/ angel), can submit an application. We scrutiny these applications closely, to avoid risk to investors. Our due diligence helps us find quality business and a good fit for the investors.

Below are a few factors that we consider for eligibility:

  • Industry
  • Product, service, or business
  • Future scope & mission
  • Fund allocation for future use
  • Market traction & growth
  • Customer engagement
  • Marketing budget
  • Team
  • Founder experience
  • Previous funding

Diligence takes around 4-5 days, and we keep you notified over mail for transparency. If the company fails our due diligence we encourage them to apply again.

Pepcorns can debar any company at any point of time, if found malafide behaviour.

Unregistered startup

How unregistered startups raise on Pepcorns?

Pepcorns allows unregistered startups to raise funds. As in India startup culture is still in an early stage with very less exposure, and so in an attempt to address this, we help outstanding ideas and startups in incubation.

On Pepcorns, unregistered startups fundraise works in two ways:

  • Purely raise through rewards (no SAFE option).
  • If your idea sounds brilliant, we help you get registered with MCA and going further shaping the idea into reality, and pushing to the larger world. For all this, we have our associated partners which help through all this.

Registered startup

How registered startups raise on Pepcorns?

Registered companies have the option to raise through SAFE with other rewards. Using SAFE companies can raise up to 10 crores from accredited investors, depending on your traction. To keep your cap table clean, we limit accredited investors' count too.

Registered companies need to qualify below criteria, to run a campaign:

  • A company must be registered under the 'Companies Act' in India.
  • A company not exceeding more than 3 years, from the date of registration.
  • Turnover not more than 25 crore INR.

HOW MUCH MONEY CAN I RAISE?

You can raise any amount up to 10 cr on Pepcorns in a particular financial year. Your campaign has two set goals (minimum and final). You can only withdraw money when you at least reach your minimum goal. This minimum goal needs to be achieved within 2 months to qualify further for the final. We suggest raising achievable targets as you can change the final amount again in the future. The success of your raise will hugely depend on the quality of your campaign and traction.

HOW CAN I PROMOTE MY CAMPAIGN?

The easiest way to get lots of potential investors to your campaign page is to send traffic from the company's website to the campaign page.
You can do this in 2 ways and direct your user and visitor from your website to the campaign page:

  • POP-UP: Visiter may click the button to visit the campaign if interested.
  • URL Redirect: You change the website DNS to point to your campaign page URL.

Social Media

If in case you decide to run ads on social media, avoid hyperbole and misleading info. You can not write 'raising funds or anything like that' instead you can simply tell about the product and ask people to support you in building the future or reframe. Do not mention any future forecast related to company growth, tho' you can mention company expansion plans and future products. Always prefer the storytelling approach instead of directly asking for support. Try approaching influencers.

Make strategy

The key to a successful rising is managing momentum. The faster the investments come in, the more new investors would want to invest. The most important thing you can do is to plan a big first day. That "pop" can spark the entire fundraise. Prepare a list of all leads who love what you are doing. Write an authentic personal email or message to them.
